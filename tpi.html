Solution for a Event management company.

This project was a part of my Object Oreinted System Analysis and design course.The students were given a hypothetical case study, of an event management company, based in New England.

We were expected to provide a solution to the case, using the OOS Analysis tools and also provide with wireframes of the final solution.

Wireframing tools used: Balsamiq.

The Case Study.
  • TPI is an event management company based in New England.
  • It uses MS Excel and other traditional tools to organise and manage their work.
  • The company wants to expand their business geographically.
  • The CEO wanted more visibility of the projects.
  • As the company was expanding, they had a tough time managing work using tradition tools.
