I started my career as a software intern at NCR Corporation India Pvt Ltd.

The internship groomed me as a web developer. User interface development and design was the part which interested me the most. I am thankful to my mentor and my first manager for giving me the liberty to try out new things and encourage my work.

My internship was converted into full-time. Now, I was a part of projects, which were more crucial for my company's business. On EDI dashboard project, I acquired business and process knowledge. It was exciting to learn how this webapp was so important, but still a cog in the big machine.

I participated in the annual innovation challenge, the idea, an android application, was appreciated and went on to be a full project.
