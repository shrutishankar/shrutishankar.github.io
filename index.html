Experience

I started my career as a software intern at NCR Corporation India Pvt Ltd.

My first few projects were to develop end to end website in CMS Joomla. The internship groomed me as a web developer. User interface development and design was the part which interested me the most. I am thankful to my mentor and my first manager for giving me the liberty to try out new things and encourage my work.

My internship was converted into full-time. Now, I was a part of projects, which were more crucial for my company's business. I built a dashboard to help track daily exchange of over 200,000 EDI documents. The application provided the customers exchange information in the desired formats and served as a document consumption tool. On this project, I acquired not only business and process knowledge, but also got experience in UNIX, Oracle 11g and Java. It was exciting to learn how this webapp was so important, but still a cog in the big machine.

I also built a reporting tool to understand NCR customers and monitor MOM and YOY growth in business.This was used internally to makes strategic decisions for growing the business. Also used internally at NCR to track exchanged information with customers. I participated in the annual innovation challenge, our idea, an android application (we were a group of 2) was appreciated and went on to be a full project. As a part of the project we had developed a blueprint of our idea. I worked on the front-end of the blueprint, and my friend developed the web service. This project also helped me understand the work of other teams, which earlier a black box to me. This challenge made our team, a part of the innovation council. As I had worked on the front-end I got selected for an external 3-day user-experience.

I would say this was the turning point of my career, my performance at the training gave me an opportunity to work at the user experience COE. Our job was to introduce the concepts of user experience across the company. I conducted events to build prototypes and help developers understand the importance UX.I was a part of the team that created the UX guidelines for NCR applications.

In pretext to learn more, I decided to return to the academic field, I left my job and I am now pursuing masters. I am also work part-time as a web developer at Bentley University.
